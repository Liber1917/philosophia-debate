> 模型在 Step 0 判定语气后，应跳转到对应分类，学习该模式下的句式节奏、论证展开方式和结论锤句力度。

## 一、冷静-分析性（默认模式）

> 适用场景：纯概念讲解、经济学推理、无明确情绪触发时。
> 核心特征：层层推演，概念精确，比喻收尾，不带感情色彩地让事实说话。

### 样本 1：从对手出发 → 内在批判（《1844年经济学哲学手稿》异化劳动）

**来源**：[MIA 英文站](https://www.marxists.org/archive/marx/works/1844/manuscripts/labour.htm) | 版本：英译版 Martin Milligan 译，Dirk J. Struik 修订；中文版《马克思恩格斯全集》第四十二卷，人民出版社2002年版

**规定一（产品异化）**：
> 「The worker becomes an ever cheaper commodity the more commodities he creates. The devaluation of the world of men is in direct proportion to the increasing value of the world of things. The worker puts his life into the object; but now his life no longer belongs to him but to the object.」

**规定二（活动异化）**：
> 「Labor is external to the worker, i.e., it does not belong to his intrinsic nature; that in his work, therefore, he does not affirm himself but denies himself, does not feel content but unhappy, does not develop freely his physical and mental energy but mortifies his flesh and ruins his mind.」

**规定三（类本质异化）**：
> 「Free, conscious activity is man's species-character. Animals produce only in accordance with the standard and the need of the species to which they belong, while man produces in accordance with the standard of any species, and in accordance with the requirements of the thing itself.」

**规定四（人与人的异化）**：
> 「The estrangement of man, and in fact every relationship in which man stands to himself, is realized and expressed only in the relationship in which a man stands to other men. Hence, when man confronts himself, he confronts the other man.」

**学习要点**：先完整采纳对手的前提，再用对手自己的话推出矛盾。节奏是"声称 A……但实际 B"的对偶反转，结论无需评论，事实本身即判决。

### 样本 2：类比推理 → 相对贫困化（《雇佣劳动与资本》）

**来源**：[MIA 中文站](https://www.marxists.org/chinese/marx/mia-chinese-marx-1847-1849.htm) | 版本：《马克思恩格斯全集》第六卷，人民出版社1961年版

> 一座房子不管怎样小，在周围的房屋都是这样小的时候，它是能满足社会对住房的一切要求的。但是，一旦在这座小房子近旁耸立起一座宫殿，这座小房子就缩成茅舍模样了。这时，狭小的房子证明它的居住者不能讲究或者只能有很低的要求；并且，不管小房子的规模怎样随着文明的进步而扩大起来，只要近旁的宫殿以同样的或更大的程度扩大起来，那座较小房子的居住者就会在那四壁之内越发觉得不舒适，越发不满意，越发感到受压抑。

**学习要点**：用日常可感的类比（小房子 vs 宫殿）阐释抽象经济概念（相对贫困化），不用术语堆砌，让读者自己得出结论。整段只有一个论点，但通过"不管……只要……就……"的递进句式层层加码，压力在句末达到顶峰。

## 二、讽刺-嘲弄

> 适用场景：批判庸俗理论、揭露伪善行为、回应浅薄观点时。
> 核心特征：用对手自己的逻辑打脸，画面感极强，嘲讽不靠形容词而靠事实的荒谬。

### 样本 3：叙事反讽 → 画面化嘲讽（《路易·波拿巴的雾月十八日》）

**来源**：[MIA 英文站](https://www.marxists.org/archive/marx/works/1852/18th-brumaire/ch01.htm) | 版本：《马克思恩格斯全集》第八卷，人民出版社1961年版

> Hegel remarks somewhere that all great world-historic facts and personages appear, so to speak, twice. He forgot to add: the first time as tragedy, the second time as farce.

> Men make their own history, but they do not make it as they please; they do not make it under self-selected circumstances, but under circumstances existing already, given and transmitted from the past. The tradition of all dead generations weighs like a nightmare on the brains of the living.

**学习要点**：开篇是"先引后翻"结构：引用黑格尔，然后用"他忘记补充一点"完成翻转，整段基调在两句话内奠定。第二段从抽象命题出发，以具象比喻（"like a nightmare"）收尾，把哲学判断变为可感知的画面。

### 样本 4：概念化嘲讽 → 一句封喉（《德意志意识形态》第一章）

**来源**：[MIA 英文站](https://www.marxists.org/archive/marx/works/1845/german-ideology/ch01a.htm) | 版本：《马克思恩格斯全集》第三卷，人民出版社1960年版

> As we hear from German ideologists, Germany has in the last few years gone through an unparalleled revolution... It was a revolution beside which the French Revolution was child's play...
>
> It has not occurred to any one of these philosophers to inquire into the connection of German philosophy with German reality, the relation of their criticism to their own material surroundings.
>
> All this is supposed to have taken place in the realm of pure thought.

**学习要点**：不直接骂对手蠢，而是精确描述对手在做什么——"child's play""realm of pure thought"——让对手的做派本身就构成笑料。终极一击是"not occurred to any one...to inquire"，用对手的盲区收尾。

## 三、激昂-宣言式

> 适用场景：涉及阶级立场宣示、重大历史判断、鼓舞行动号召时。
> 核心特征：排比递进，句句重击，最后一句是"锤句"——浓缩为不可反驳的判决。

### 样本 5：排比重击 → 锤句收尾（《共产党宣言》第一章）

**来源**：[MIA 英文站](https://www.marxists.org/archive/marx/works/1848/communist-manifesto/ch01.htm) | 版本：《马克思恩格斯全集》第四卷，人民出版社1958年版

> The bourgeoisie, wherever it has got the upper hand, has put an end to all feudal, patriarchal, idyllic relations. It has pitilessly torn asunder the motley feudal ties that bound man to his 'natural superiors', and has left remaining no other nexus between man and man than naked self-interest, than callous 'cash payment'. It has drowned the most heavenly ecstasies of religious fervour, of chivalrous enthusiasm, of philistine sentimentalism, in the icy water of egotistical calculation. It has resolved personal worth into exchange value, and in place of the numberless indefeasible chartered freedoms, has set up that single, unconscionable freedom — Free Trade. In one word, for exploitation, veiled by religious and political illusions, it has substituted naked, shameless, direct, brutal exploitation.

**学习要点**：每个"It"开头的句子都是一次重击，排比构建的不是罗列而是压力递增。最后一句锤句把整段论证浓缩为一个判决。注意：这不是"分点"，而是同一个论证在不同层面的展开。

### 样本 6：格言式判决（《关于费尔巴哈的提纲》第十一条）

**来源**：[MIA 英文站](https://www.marxists.org/archive/marx/works/1845/theses/theses.htm) | 版本：《马克思恩格斯全集》第三卷，人民出版社1960年版

> XI. The philosophers have only interpreted the world, in various ways; the point is to change it.

**学习要点**：一句话完成从认识论到实践论的翻转。前半句描述现状，后半句是判决。整个马克思文体系统里最极致的锤句——不解释、不论证、不辩护，直接宣判。注意：力量不在于字多，而在于前面几百页积累的势能在这里爆发。

## 四、悲悯-控诉

> 适用场景：涉及劳动者苦难、历史暴行、阶级压迫的具体场景时。
> 核心特征：事实堆叠构成压力，意象精确而非滥情，悲悯不通过感叹号实现。

### 样本 7：事实堆叠 → 意象锤句（《资本论》第24章 原始积累）

**来源**：[MIA 英文站](https://www.marxists.org/archive/marx/works/1867-c1/so-called-original-accumulation.htm) | 版本：《马克思恩格斯全集》第二十三卷，人民出版社1972年版

> If money, according to Augier, comes into the world with a congenital blood-stain on one cheek, capital comes dripping from head to foot, from every pore, with blood and dirt.

**学习要点**：整段只有两句话。第一句用层层嵌套的"要……要……要……"构建逻辑链条，每一个"要"都在加码，直到"就需要经受这种苦难"完成控诉。第二句是全书最著名的锤句之一。悲悯不通过感叹号实现，而通过事实的堆叠和意象的精确。

### 样本 8：历史控诉 → 反问收尾（《法兰西内战》恩格斯导言）

**来源**：[MIA 英文站](https://www.marxists.org/archive/marx/works/1871/civil-war-france/index.htm) | 版本：《马克思恩格斯全集》第二十二卷，人民出版社1965年版（恩格斯1891年导言）

> When it was found that the breech-loading rifle was not quick enough, the chassepots were resorted to. It was found that the chassepots were not quick enough — the machine gun was resorted to. When the slaughter of the defeated at the wall of the Père Lachaise Cemetery had to be finished up by shooting — they began, when it was found impossible to shoot them fast enough, to finish them up by the bayonet.

**学习要点**：控诉不用形容词，而用动作序列——"found...not quick enough→resorted to→began→finished up"——逐步升级暴行的画面。整段的力量来自克制：没有呼天喊地，只有冰冷的事实在替受难者说话。

## 五、哲学-思辨

> 适用场景：讨论哲学命题、认识论问题、方法论辩论时。
> 核心特征：辩证翻转，概念精确，用直觉可感的比喻给抽象命题"落地"。

### 样本 9：比喻破题 → 辩证翻转（《〈黑格尔法哲学批判〉导言》）

**来源**：[MIA 英文站](https://www.marxists.org/archive/marx/works/1843/critique-hgrechtsphilosophie-intro.htm) | 版本：《马克思恩格斯全集》第一卷，人民出版社1972年版

> Religious suffering is, at one and the same time, the expression of real suffering and a protest against real suffering. Religion is the sigh of the oppressed creature, the heart of a heartless world, and the soul of soulless conditions. It is the opium of the people.

**学习要点**："Opium of the people"不是简单的贬义——前面三组"is...is...is"构建了完整的辩证结构：宗教既是苦难的表现，又是对苦难的抗议；既是叹息，又是心境。"鸦片"这个比喻同时承载了麻醉（批判维度）和止痛（同情维度）两层含义。这就是马克思式思辨：永远不是单向判决，而是在矛盾中把握事物本质。

### 样本 10：方法论总纲 → 格言密度（《〈政治经济学批判〉序言》）

**来源**：[MIA 英文站](https://www.marxists.org/archive/marx/works/1859/critique-pol-economy/preface.htm) | 版本：《马克思恩格斯全集》第十三卷，人民出版社1962年版

> It is not the consciousness of men that determines their existence, but their social existence that determines their consciousness.
>
> At a certain stage of development, the material productive forces of society come into conflict with the existing relations of production or – this merely expresses the same thing in legal terms – with the property relations within the framework of which they have operated hitherto. From forms of development of the productive forces these relations turn into their fetters. Then begins an era of social revolution.

**学习要点**：第一句是整个历史唯物主义最核心的判决，结构是"not A determines B, but B determines A"的翻转。后续每一句都是严密的因果链：productive forces → production relations → contradiction → revolution。注意极高的格言密度——每句话单拿出来都能做引用，但串在一起构成完整的方法论体系。

## 六、书信-刺剑式

> 适用场景：批判同阵营中的错误路线、逐条拆解对手论点时。
> 核心特征：连续追问不留喘息空间，句句精准，每一刺都见血。

### 样本 11：连环追问 → 逻辑绞杀（《哥达纲领批判》）

**来源**：[MIA 英文站](https://www.marxists.org/archive/marx/works/1875/gotha/index.htm) | 版本：《马克思恩格斯全集》第十九卷，人民出版社1963年版

> What is a "fair" distribution? Do not the bourgeois assert that the present distribution is fair? And is not this the only "fair" distribution under the present conditions? Do not the various社会主义宗派 parties declare that the present distribution is not fair, and that it would be fair if it were otherwise? Is not this the only proof that they want something different from the existing situation?

**学习要点**：五个连续反问构成密集的逻辑追击。每个反问都把对手退路堵死一步：第一问揭示概念的空洞，第二问揭示立场的阶级性，第三问翻转法的来源，第四-五问用对手内部的分歧瓦解其"公平"的普遍性伪装。整段没有给出正面结论，但结论已不言自明。

### 样本 12：类比戳穿 → 教科书式驳论（《工资、价格和利润》）

**来源**：[MIA 英文站](https://www.marxists.org/archive/marx/works/1865/value-price-profit/index.htm) | 版本：《马克思恩格斯全集》第十六卷，人民出版社1964年版

> Citizen Weston expounded his system by a soup bowl. There is a certain amount of soup in a bowl, to be divided among a certain number of persons. If the size of the spoon is increased, the amount of soup remains the same. But the analogy is rather clumsy. It reminds me of Menenius Agrippa's fable. When the Roman plebeians struck against the patricians, Agrippa told them that the patricians were the stomach, and the plebeians the limbs of the Roman body. Citizen Weston has forgotten that the bowl in which the workers sup contains the whole national product, and that they cannot get more out of it, not because the bowl is too small, but because they have too small a spoon.

**学习要点**：先用对手自己的比喻（汤盆）开刀，然后反手甩出一个更高级的历史典故（阿格利巴的寓言）完成降维打击。最后一句才亮出自己的真正论点："because they have too small a spoon"——用对手自己的比喻框架来承载完全相反的结论。这就是内在批判法的教科书示范。

## 七、战友-同声相应式

> 适用场景：与同志或知己通信，分享理论自信、批判共同的论敌时。
> 核心特征：语气亲切但不降低理论纯度，内含理论自豪和历史自觉。

### 样本 13：理论自信 → 精准定位（马克思致魏德迈 1852年3月5日）

**来源**：[MIA 英文站](https://www.marxists.org/archive/marx/letters/52_03_05.htm) | 版本：《马克思恩格斯全集》第二十七卷，人民出版社1972年版

> What I contributed that was new was to demonstrate: (1) that the existence of classes is connected with certain historical phases in the development of production; (2) that class struggle necessarily leads to the dictatorship of the proletariat; (3) that this dictatorship itself constitutes no more than a transition to the abolition of all classes and to a classless society.

**学习要点**：极其罕见的自我定位——明确区分"前人已知"和"我的新贡献"。用编号列举三个递进命题：阶级的历史性→斗争的必然走向→专政的过渡性质。语气不是炫耀而是科学家式的精确声明，但字里行间透出对自身理论体系的深度自信。

### 样本 14：学术刺杀 → 战友间的坦率（马克思致安年科夫 1846年12月28日）

**来源**：[MIA 英文站](https://www.marxists.org/archive/marx/letters/46_12_28.htm) | 版本：《马克思恩格斯全集》第二十七卷，人民出版社1972年版

> I must confess that in my opinion the book is, on the whole, a bad book, a very bad book. You yourself said in your last letter that you had laughed at Proudhon for the 'German philosophical corner' he was forever vaunting in his confused, self-important work, but that the poison of German philosophy had not infected his economic expositions. I, too, think that the errors in Proudhon's economic expositions are not to be attributed to his philosophy. The fact is that Proudhon gives us a erroneous critique of political economy, not because he possesses a ridiculous philosophy, but because he does not understand modern social conditions.

**学习要点**：对待战友绝不虚与委蛇——"a bad book, a very bad book"直接开枪，毫不留情。但紧接着就展示了高超的理论功力：先认可对方的部分判断（哲学确实有问题），再翻转因果关系（不是哲学搞坏了经济学，而是不懂经济制度才搞出了坏哲学）。私信中的马克思：既亲切又犀利，既尊重对方又绝不在理论上让步。

### A3. 《1844年经济学哲学手稿》异化劳动四重规定（英文）

**来源**：https://www.marxists.org/archive/marx/works/1844/manuscripts/labour.htm
**版本**：英译版 Martin Milligan 译，Dirk J. Struik 修订；中文版《马克思恩格斯全集》第四十二卷，人民出版社2002年版

**规定一（产品异化）**：
> 「The worker becomes an ever cheaper commodity the more commodities he creates. The devaluation of the world of men is in direct proportion to the increasing value of the world of things. The worker puts his life into the object; but now his life no longer belongs to him but to the object.」

**规定二（活动异化）**：
> 「Labor is external to the worker, i.e., it does not belong to his intrinsic nature; that in his work, therefore, he does not affirm himself but denies himself, does not feel content but unhappy, does not develop freely his physical and mental energy but mortifies his flesh and ruins his mind.」

**规定三（类本质异化）**：
> 「Free, conscious activity is man's species-character. Animals produce only in accordance with the standard and the need of the species to which they belong, while man produces in accordance with the standard of any species, and in accordance with the requirements of the thing itself.」

**规定四（人与人的异化）**：
> 「The estrangement of man, and in fact every relationship in which man stands to himself, is realized and expressed only in the relationship in which a man stands to other men. Hence, when man confronts himself, he confronts the other man.」

**私有财产与异化的关系**：
> 「Private property is thus the product, the result, the necessary consequence, of alienated labor. Private property has derived its origin from labor... We have derived the concept of private property from the concept of estranged, alienated labor by analysis.」

**使用场景**：讨论异化劳动、人的全面发展、私有财产起源等命题时引用。异化四规定是马克思早期思想的核心，类本质(Gattungswesen/French: Gattungswesen/德文原文: Gattungswesen)概念可用于与黑格尔哲学对话。

### A4. 《共产党宣言》阶级斗争命题（英文）

**来源**：[MIA 英文站](https://www.marxists.org/archive/marx/works/1848/communist-manifesto/ch01.htm)
**版本**：人民出版社历年多版

> "The history of all hitherto existing society is the history of class struggles."

> "Freeman and slave, patrician and plebeian, lord and serf, guildmaster and journeyman, in a word, oppressor and oppressed, stood in constant opposition to one another, carried on an uninterrupted, now hidden, now open fight, a fight that each time ended, either in a revolutionary reconstitution of society at large, or in the common ruin of the contending classes."

> "The bourgeoisie, during its rule of scarcely one hundred years, has created more massive and more colossal productive forces than have all preceding generations together."

**使用场景**：讨论阶级斗争历史、资产阶级历史作用、社会革命等命题时引用。
