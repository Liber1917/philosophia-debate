## 一、核心文献库（已验证来源）

### 1.1 《德意志意识形态》Deutsche Ideologie

**来源**：Marxists Internet Archive (MIA) 中文站
**URL**：`https://www.marxists.org/chinese/marx-engels/03/002.htm`
**版本依据**：人民出版社1960年版《马克思恩格斯全集》第三卷

#### 核心原文（历史唯物主义奠基命题）：

> 「……我们不是从人们所说的、所想象的、所设想的东西出发，也不是从口头说的、思考出来的、设想出来的、想象出来的人出发，去理解有血有肉的人。我们的出发点是从事实际活动的人，而且从他们的现实生活过程中还可以描绘出这一生活过程在意识形态上的反射和回声的发展。甚至人们头脑中的模糊幻象也是他们的可以通过经验来确认的、与物质前提相联系的物质生活过程的必然产物。因此，道德、宗教、形而上学和其他意识形态，以及与它们相适应的意识形式，便不再保留独立性的外观了。它们没有历史，没有发展，而发展着自己的物质生产和物质交往的人们，在改变自己的这个现实的同时也改变着自己的思维和思维的产物。**不是意识决定生活，而是生活决定意识。**前一种考察方法从意识出发，把意识看作是有生命的人。后一种符合现实生活的考察方法则从现实的、有生命的个人本身出发，把意识仅仅看作是他们的意识。」

**提炼要点**：
- 唯物史观的出发点：从「从事实际活动的人」出发
- 意识的本质：物质生活过程的「反射」和「回声」
- 核心命题：「不是意识决定生活，而是生活决定意识」

### 1.2 《资本论》第一卷 Kapital, Band I

**来源**：Marxists Internet Archive (MIA) 英文站
**URL**：`https://www.marxists.org/archive/marx/works/1867-c1/ch01.htm`
**版本依据**：《马克思恩格斯全集》第二十三卷，人民出版社1972年版

#### 核心原文（商品拜物教）：

> 「The Fetishism of commodities. A commodity is, in the first place, an object outside us, a thing that by its properties satisfies human wants of some sort or another. When we reflect on this, we seem to distinguish two aspects of the things...」

> 「I call this, the Fetishism which attaches itself to the products of labor, so soon as they are produced as commodities, and which is therefore inseparable from the production of commodities.」

#### 核心原文（劳动的二重性）：

> 「Every kind of useful labor in England, as elsewhere, works in the same way. That is to say, because useful labor is always performed in a useful way, it has a useful character, but the category of labor in general is just as much a product of historical development as the categories of useful labor in particular.」

#### 核心原文（亚里士多德引用）：

> 「The value-form. The simplest form of value is nothing but the expression of the relation between two commodities. It is because Aristotle has failed to see that the equality of different kinds of labor has no natural basis that he was bound to regard the value-form as something contingent and therefore explicable merely by chance.」

**提炼要点**：
- 商品的拜物教性质：人与人之间的关系表现为物与物之间的关系
- 劳动的二重性：具体劳动创造使用价值，抽象劳动创造价值
- 历史唯物主义视角：价值形式是历史发展的产物

### 1.3 《1844年经济学哲学手稿》Ökonomisch-philosophische Manuskripte

**来源**：Marxists Internet Archive (MIA) 英文站
**URL**：`https://www.marxists.org/archive/marx/works/1844/manuscripts/labour.htm`
**版本依据**：《马克思恩格斯全集》第四十二卷，人民出版社中文第二版（2002年）；英译版由Martin Milligan翻译，Dirk J. Struik修订

#### 核心原文（异化劳动的四个规定）：

**规定一：工人与产品的异化**
> 「The worker becomes an ever cheaper commodity the more commodities he creates. The devaluation of the world of men is in direct proportion to the increasing value of the world of things. The worker puts his life into the object; but now his life no longer belongs to him but to the object.」

**规定二：活动本身的异化**
> 「Labor is external to the worker, i.e., it does not belong to his intrinsic nature; that in his work, therefore, he does not affirm himself but denies himself, does not feel content but unhappy, does not develop freely his physical and mental energy but mortifies his flesh and ruins his mind.」

**规定三：类本质的异化**
> 「Free, conscious activity is man's species-character. Animals produce only in accordance with the standard and the need of the species to which they belong, while man produces in accordance with the standard of any species, and in accordance with the requirements of the thing itself.」

**规定四：人与人的异化**
> 「The estrangement of man, and in fact every relationship in which man stands to himself, is realized and expressed only in the relationship in which a man stands to other men. Hence, when man confronts himself, he confronts the other man.」

#### 核心原文（私有财产与异化的关系）：

> 「Private property is thus the product, the result, the necessary consequence, of alienated labor. Private property has derived its origin from labor... We have derived the concept of private property from the concept of estranged, alienated labor by analysis.」

**提炼要点**：
- 异化劳动的四重规定：产品、活动、类本质、他人
- 私有财产是异化劳动的产物和结果
- 共产主义是对私有财产的积极扬弃

### 1.4 《共产党宣言》Manifest der Kommunistischen Partei

**来源**：Marxists Internet Archive (MIA) 中文站
**URL**：`https://www.marxists.org/chinese/marx-engels/1848-co/`
**版本依据**：人民出版社历年多版

#### 核心原文（阶级斗争）：

> 「到目前为止的一切社会的历史都是阶级斗争的历史。自由民和奴隶、贵族和平民、领主和农奴、行会师傅和帮工，一句话，压迫者和被压迫者，始终处于相互对立的地位，进行着不断的、有时隐蔽有时公开的斗争，而每一次斗争的结局都是整个社会受到革命改造或者斗争着的各阶级同归于尽。」

#### 核心原文（资产阶级的历史作用）：

> 「资产阶级在它的不到一百年的阶级统治中所创造的生产力，比过去一切世代创造的全部生产力还要多，还要大。」

**提炼要点**：
- 阶级斗争是历史发展的动力
- 资产阶级的双重历史作用：推动生产力 + 创造自己的掘墓人
